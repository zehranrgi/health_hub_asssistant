CVS PHARMACY SERVICES AND INFORMATION

=== PRESCRIPTION SERVICES ===

Prescription Filling:
- Most prescriptions ready in 15-30 minutes
- Text notifications when ready for pickup
- Auto-refill program available for maintenance medications
- 90-day supply available for chronic conditions (often lower copay)
- Prescription transfer: Call or online, we contact your old pharmacy

Delivery Options:
- Same-day delivery available in select locations
- Free 1-2 day delivery on prescriptions
- Mail order pharmacy for long-term medications
- Drive-thru service at most locations

24/7 Services:
- Many CVS locations open 24 hours
- Pharmacy hours may vary - check CVS.com/store-locator
- 24/7 prescription refill by phone or app
- After-hours prescription pickup with CVS Pharmacy app

Digital Tools:
- CVS Pharmacy app for refills, reminders, medication tracking
- Text refill: Text prescription number to 55495
- Online account management at CVS.com
- Virtual pharmacist consultations

Cost Savings:
- Generic medications available (typically 70-80% cheaper)
- ExtraCare Health card for prescription rewards
- Price match guarantee
- GoodRx coupons accepted
- Manufacturer copay cards accepted

=== IMMUNIZATION SERVICES ===

Available Vaccines:
- Flu shots (standard and high-dose for 65+)
- COVID-19 vaccines and boosters
- Shingles (Shingrix)
- Pneumonia (PCV20, PPSV23)
- Tdap/Td (tetanus)
- Hepatitis A & B
- HPV (ages 9-45)
- Meningitis
- MMR
- Travel vaccines (at MinuteClinic)

Vaccine Services:
- Walk-ins welcome (wait times may vary)
- Online scheduling recommended
- Appointments available 7 days a week
- Most vaccines covered 100% by insurance
- Records uploaded to state immunization registry
- Vaccine cards provided

Who Can Vaccinate:
- Pharmacists can vaccinate ages 5+ (varies by state)
- MinuteClinic vaccinates all ages including infants
